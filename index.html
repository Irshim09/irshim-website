About Us

Founded in 1988, IRSHIM began as a trading company specializing in construction steel materials. With a commitment to growth and innovation, we soon established our own manufacturing plant, laying the foundation for a broader industrial presence.

Our strategic partnership with CNBM, a leading Chinese state-owned enterprise, played a key role in accelerating our development and expanding our operations across the region.

To meet the rising demand in the Gulf, IRSHIM extended its business reach through collaborations with South Korean industrial giants such as POSCO, Hyundai, and Samsung.

Building on this momentum, IRSHIM entered the oilfield services sector, becoming a trusted supplier to major energy companies.
